Our Story

The Daily Grind opened its doors in 2018 with one simple belief: that great coffee should come with a great atmosphere. Founded by two friends who were tired of choosing between quality and comformt, we set out to build a space where both could coexist.

We source our beans directly from small farms across ethiopia, colombia, and rwanda - farmers who care as much about thier craft as we do about ours.

Meet the Team

Sourcing & sustainabilty

We are committed to ethical sourcing. Every bean we buy comes with full traceability, fair pricing to the farmer, and zero middlemen. Our cups and packaging are compostable, and we run a bring-your-own-cup discount programme.
