FAQs

Do you have Wi-Fi?

Yes — free Wi-Fi for all customers. Ask at the counter for the password.

Do you have vegan options?

Absolutely. Oat milk, almond milk, and soy milk are available. Several food items are vegan — just ask our staff.

Is there parking?

Yes, free parking is available directly in front of the shop and in the municipal lot next door.

Can I book the space for a private event?

We do offer after-hours bookings for private events. Use the contact form above or email us directly.
